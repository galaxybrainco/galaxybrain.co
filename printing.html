Printing at Galaxy Brain

Galaxy Brain primarily prints production work on our Risograph EZ221U, which we have lovingly named Animal. Animal is something of a picky eater, and accepts paper with the following characteristics:

  • Maximum dimensions 11" x 17", aka Ledger, Tabloid, A3
  • Paper weight from newsprint up to 80lb cover stock.
  • No finish of any kind. Animal needs paper with teeth.

Animal does not print full-bleed. We can trim down projects to give the appearance of full-bleed. Animal's max printing area is 8 1/4" x 14 1/16"

Animal runs on official Riso brand ink, which Galaxy Brain stocks in the following colors:

  • Black
  • Bright Red
  • Green
  • Risofederal Blue a.k.a. Fed Blue
  • Yellow

If you are publishing with Galaxy Brain, all print costs are assumed by Galaxy Brain. If you are interested in publishing with Galaxy Brain or working with Galaxy Brain on a print-for-hire project, please email hello@galaxybrain.co.
