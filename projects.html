Projects by Galaxy Brain

  • Adult Juice Box is our flagship zine and the project that started Galaxy Brain. It’s mostly about wine, beer, and spirits, and can be found in bookstores and bottle shops around and about San Francisco.
  • Thought and a Chaser is an irregular podcase about libations, language, and logistics. We’re working our way through the lexicon as we discover fascinating and strange stories about how our favorite beverages are made.
  • VictoryPic is a Slack app for taking selfies. In the wake of COVID-19, we realized we wanted to see each other’s faces more in our Slack channel, so we made an app for doing so. We’re not sure about how it’s impacted our productivity, but we’re certainly happier.
